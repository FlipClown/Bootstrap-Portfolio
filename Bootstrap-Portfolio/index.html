Me Stuff

I like long walks on the beach. Food is life. MMORPG's and anime are my weaknesses. When I was only five years old I watched Dragon Ball Z for the first time. I didn't even know how to speak engish at that age. I remember being dumbfounded at the show. It was the episode when Son Gohan and the Supreme Kai were trying to confront Babadi and Dabura, in order to prevent Majin Buu's resurrection. Meanwhile, Son Goku and Vegeta were dueling it out to see who was stronger. I've never seen a show like this before. It sparked my love for anime.


Someday, I want to develop a webapp, or video game, that'll be forever be remembered. The first MMO I ever played was Runescape. I was about 13 years old when I first played it. I didn't like it all that much in the beginning. I didn't know what do, and what skills to work on. I couldn't even figure out how level up my attack stat. However, after a while, I learned the ins and outs of the game. Ever since then, I've played other RPG's like World of Warcraft, Destiny, Monster Hunter, etc.
